Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Anchor Launcher ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Anchor Launcher Android application globally and complies with major international privacy frameworks, including the GDPR (Europe), CCPA (California), and others.

Anchor is designed as a premium, privacy-first productivity launcher. We believe your device data belongs to you. Most processing happens locally on your device.

2. Device Permissions & Data Usage

To provide a seamless home screen experience, Anchor requires certain Android permissions. Here is exactly why we need them and how the data is used:

📦

App Packages

QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES

Why: To display your installed apps in the app drawer and allow you to launch, search, and manage them.

Privacy: We only read the list of installed applications locally. This data never leaves your device.

📍

Location

ACCESS_COARSE/FINE_LOCATION

Why: To provide accurate, local weather updates directly on your home screen.

Privacy: Location is fetched securely to retrieve weather data and is cached locally. We do not track your location history.

📇

Contacts

READ_CONTACTS

Why: To allow you to search for contacts directly from your home screen and use quick-dial shortcuts.

Privacy: Contact data is indexed and searched exclusively on your device.

🖼️

System UI

SET_WALLPAPER & BIND_APPWIDGET

Why: To allow you to change your wallpaper and add your favorite widgets to the home screen.

3. Data Collection and Sharing

We do not sell your data. Anchor Launcher operates with a local-first philosophy.

  • No Tracking: We do not include third-party advertising trackers.
  • Third-Party APIs: To provide live weather updates, the app directly contacts the Open-Meteo API from your device using your location coordinates. Open-Meteo is a privacy-friendly service that does not track users or sell data. We do not intermediate this request or log your location on our servers.
  • Data Sharing: We do not share your personal information, installed apps list, or contacts with any third parties.

4. Global Privacy Rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

Depending on your location, you have rights concerning your data. However, because Anchor Launcher processes your data locally on your device, you are entirely in control:

  • Right to Access & Delete: You can clear the app's cache or uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored data (like cached weather or app preferences).
  • Right to Opt-Out: Since we do not sell your data, there is no need to opt-out of data sales.
  • Revoking Permissions: You can revoke Contacts or Location permissions at any time via Android System Settings. The launcher will continue to work, though specific features (like weather or contact search) will be disabled.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect or solicit data from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible.
